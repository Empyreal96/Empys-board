Windows 10 Mobile Adaptation Kits

A small collection of leaked WPAKs for 14xxx and 16212 Builds of W10M

Notes:

  • Updates will be Flight Signed. You will have to enable Flight Signing on your device, and use a specific date when installing
  • Tools of your choice will be required. To filter and push the required cabs for your device, I recommend using Universal Updater by Fadil Fadz. You can also manually use IUTool and GetDuLogs to fetch Installed Packages in the device and match up what you need to flash. (I may write a guide for this in the future)
  • Accept Risk. As with ANY prerelease build, there is always a slim chance of breaking something. If you are not confident in pushing offline cabs to your device, don't proceed.
